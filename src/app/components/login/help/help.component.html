Ignore this message if you have already updated your password.

Step 2: Enter your email id.
Step 3: You will receive a password reset link by email. (Check spam box, in case you did not receive the email in inbox.)
Step 4: Click on the password reset link and enter your new password.
Step 5: Login to your new account.
